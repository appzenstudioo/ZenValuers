Our Valuation Process

Transparent steps that produce reliable, bank-accepted valuation reports.

How We Work — Step by Step

1

Initial Enquiry

Contact us via the form or phone with basic property details. We confirm scope, timeframe and fee estimate.

2

Engagement & Site Visit

We conduct a physical inspection, record measurements and photographs, and collect documents.

3

Research & Analysis

Market comparables, rental data and cost/income assumptions are gathered and reconciled to determine value.

4

Report Preparation

We prepare a clear, evidence-backed valuation report with methodology, conclusions and supporting exhibits suitable for banks and legal use.

5

Delivery & Clarification

Final report delivered electronically. We provide clarifications and follow-up support for lenders or auditors if required.
