Our Valuation Services

Accurate, compliant and bank-approved property valuation services across India.

Residential Valuation

Valuation for apartments, villas and independent houses for sale, loan and legal purposes. We provide clear evidence, comparable sales and measured drawings where required.

Purpose

  • Bank home loans and balance-sheet reporting
  • Sale / purchase negotiations
  • Income tax and wealth assessment

Documents Required

  • Title deed / sale deed
  • Approved plan & occupancy certificate
  • Property tax / khata / EC
  • Photographs and sale agreement (if available)

Commercial Valuation

Valuation of offices, retail, warehouses and industrial units using market, income and residual approaches depending on the asset type.

Purpose

  • Bank & NBFC lending
  • Lease & rental assessment
  • Investment and balance-sheet reporting

Documents Required

  • Title and ownership documents
  • Approved plans and lease agreements
  • Rent rolls (if leased)

Land & Plot Valuation

Site-specific assessments accounting for zoning, development potential, FAR, and comparable sales to determine market value.

Purpose

  • Sale / purchase & development appraisal
  • Bank loan against land
  • Legal and taxation matters

Documents Required

  • RTC / land records and survey sketch
  • Conversion order (if applicable)
  • Title deed

Bank Loan Valuation

Bank-accepted reports prepared with clear scope, methodology and supporting evidence to meet lender requirements and audit checks.

Highlights

  • IBBI-compliant methodology
  • Physical site inspection & photographs
  • Comparable evidence & reconciliation
